Real estate investment trusts, royalty streams, and dividend-bearing assets are being tokenised at record pace. Yet the holders of these tokens face a fundamental problem: their assets are productive, but illiquid.

They cannot participate in DeFi liquidity markets. They cannot lever their positions. They cannot access stablecoin capital without liquidating underlying holdings.

RWA Collateral Service resolves this. By treating Liquid Staked Tokens as collateral against a proven dual-oracle valuation model, we give RWA holders access to stablecoin liquidity while retaining their exposure — and give liquidity providers a new, yield-bearing venue backed by tangible assets.

Protocol Mechanics

Three Roles. One Protocol.

01
Borrowers

Lock & Borrow

RWA LST holders deposit their tokens as collateral in the Collateral Vault. They receive stablecoins immediately — up to a protocol-defined loan-to-value ratio — while retaining economic exposure to the underlying asset's income stream and appreciation.

02
Liquidity Providers

Deposit & Earn

Investors deposit stablecoins into the Stablecoin Pool, receiving pro-rata shares. Every borrowing position generates opening and closing fees, which flow back to the pool — increasing the redemption value of all shares over time.

03
Liquidators

Bid & Acquire

When a position's health factor falls below the liquidation threshold, LSTs transfer to the Disposal Contract. Pre-crediting investors compete in ascending auctions for the liquidated collateral, clearing bad debt and acquiring tokenised RWAs at competitive prices.

Oracle Design

Dual-Valuation Oracle:
Capturing Every Dimension of RWA Value

Real-World Assets derive value from two distinct, independently variable sources. Our RWA Valuation Oracle captures both — and applies governance-adjustable risk haircuts to each, enabling nuanced, asset-specific risk management.

Emissions Oracle

Reports the present value of the underlying asset's income stream entitlement — rent, royalties, or dividends. Updated by authorised keeper oracles with per-token precision.

Rent Royalties Dividends

NPV Oracle

Provides the Net Present Value — the discounted fair value of the physical or legal asset itself. Can be sourced from licensed valuation agents or Chainlink feeds.

Property Value Asset Appraisal

RWA Valuation Oracle — Aggregation Formula

totalValuePerToken =
  emissionsPerToken × emissionsWeight
  + npvPerToken × npvWeight

// Weights default to 10,000 bp (100%)
// Governance may apply risk haircuts

AdminController

Governance of all protocol parameters: fees, collateral threshold, liquidation threshold, and auction duration. Owner-only access.

CollateralVault

Core contract managing position lifecycle: open, close, top-up, harvest, and liquidate operations for all RWA LST collateral.

StablecoinPool

Share-based LP pool (ERC-4626 inspired) where investors deposit stablecoins. Earns pro-rata fees from every position open and close.

DisposalContract

Handles liquidation auctions. Investors pre-credit funds; the winning bidder receives LSTs and outstanding debt is repaid to the pool.

RWAValuationOracle

Aggregates Emissions and NPV oracles into a single USD price per token. Supports governance-adjustable per-token risk haircuts.

Investors
05

Auction & Resolution

Investors bid ascending. Winning bid clears debt to pool. Surplus (if any) returned to original borrower. Winner receives LSTs.

Health Factor = (Collateral Value USD × 10,000) / Loan Amount — expressed in basis points. Open minimum: 15,000 bp (150%). Liquidation threshold: 11,000 bp (110%).

Oracle Security Roadmap

Testnet uses stub oracles configurable by the owner. The production path replaces these with Chainlink Data Feeds or multi-sig credentialled keeper oracles — ensuring tamper-resistant, manipulation-resistant valuation.
